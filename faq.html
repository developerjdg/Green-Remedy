At Green Remedy we maintain both medical and adult-use licenses so we are able to serve those that meet the following requirements:

To be a valid medical patient, we require a valid government-issued photo identification card, a valid medical recommendation, and you must be at least 18 years of age or older.

To qualify as a recreational client, you must provide a valid government-issued photo identification card and be 21 years of age or older.

In accordance with state and local laws, the daily purchase limit amount is 1oz of cannabis per person.

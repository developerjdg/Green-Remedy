ABOUT GREEN REMEDY

Established in 2009 by a group of local medical cannabis users and caregivers, Green Remedy is a reputable cannabis dispensary in Richmond, California. Green Remedy caters to both medical and recreational consumers to offer dependable, safe access to premium cannabis products. It has developed into a well-known brand in the cannabis industry over time, renowned for its dedication to community involvement, education, and quality.

The dispensary sells a wide variety of high-quality cannabis items, such as edibles, concentrates, vaporizers, accessories, and flower. Green Remedy ensures that customers have access to premium options by partnering with reputable cannabis companies such as Wyld and Stiiizy.

At the heart of Green Remedy is its knowledgeable and compassionate staff, dedicated to helping customers find the right products for their individual needs. Beyond simply providing cannabis, Green Remedy is committed to educating the community about responsible cannabis use, fostering a welcoming and supportive environment for all visitors.

Located at 2928 Hilltop Mall Road, Richmond, CA, Green Remedy continues to thrive by staying true to its core values: safety, care, quality, and community.

Our Mission

At Green Remedy, our mission is to offer safe, reliable, and compassionate access to premium cannabis in a welcoming, professional environment. Founded by patients and caregivers, we are committed to serving both medical and recreational users with integrity, personalised care, and a strong focus on education. We believe in empowering our community through knowledge and supporting responsible cannabis use. Every product we offer is carefully selected to ensure quality, safety, and effectiveness. Our goal is to promote wellness, reduce stigma, and help individuals enhance their quality of life through trusted, high-quality cannabis solutions.

Our Values

At Green Remedy, our goal is to be a highly regarded leader in the cannabis sector, known for our unwavering dedication to community, compassion, and integrity, along with offering high-quality products. We envision a future where cannabis is widely recognised and embraced as a natural tool for balance, healing, and wellness in daily life. As early supporters of the cannabis movement, we've seen firsthand the transformative power of this plant, and we aim to create a dispensary model that goes beyond the transactional experience.

We are committed to promoting a culture of education, transparency, and responsible use, working to reduce stigma and position cannabis as a key element of a healthy, holistic lifestyle. Through ongoing community engagement, consumer education, and collaborations with ethical brands, we empower our customers to make informed, confident decisions that support their well-being. Our dispensary is a space where people from all walks of life can feel comfortable, educated, and encouraged as they explore cannabis.
Green Remedy also aims to build a vibrant, inclusive cannabis community where everyone, whether seasoned users or newcomers, feels welcomed and supported. We are continuously looking for innovative ways to create a positive impact, from adopting new technologies and sustainable practices to expanding access in underserved communities. Our vision is guided by a single principle: to make cannabis a respected, accessible, and valued part of modern health and culture, moving towards a more conscious cannabis future.
